Friðsæll og notalegur staður með snyrtilegum salernum, heitu og köldu vatni og rafmagni. Þvottavél, þurrkari og sturta er einnig á staðnum. Stutt er í alls kyns afþreyingu. Á tjaldsvæðinu sjálfu er svokallaður Ærslabelgur þar sem tugir barna geta skemmt sér tímum saman. Tveir fótboltavellir eru við tjaldsvæðið og aðeins 250 m. ganga eftir fallegum skógarstíg að sundlaug.

A new camping ground was taken into operation in Varmahlid in the summer of 2008. The facilities are also very good with hot and cold water, showers five toilets and hand basins both inside and outside. We also have good access for disabled guests. The area is divided into separate areas for tents, caravans and campervans all with electricity. Barbeques are available for guests and 250 meters through the forest you come to a lovely swimming pool, a playing area for the children is also in the area and 2 football fields are a stone throw away.
