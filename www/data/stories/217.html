The three-star Hotel Varmahlíð is located right by the Ring Road, National Road No. 1. The hotel’s excellent facilities include 19 rooms which can accommodate up to 38 people at once. Arriving guests are received by the hotel manager, Svanhildur Pálsdóttir, and the rest of the staff who work to uphold the hotel’s reputation of fine service and hospitality. A home-style breakfast buffet awaits overnight guests every morning, while in the summer season the restaurant offers an evening á la carte menu for guests and passers-by alike.

Velkomin á Hótel Varmahlíð í hjarta Skagafjarðar Þriggja stjörnu hótel þægilega staðsett á spennandi áfangastað. Þar er boðið upp á gistingu í 19 huggulegum og velbúnum herbergjum.
